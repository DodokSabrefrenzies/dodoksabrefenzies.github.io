About me

Some words about me

Welcome everyone! My name is Jerod aka The8BitDrummer (Or 8-Bit for short)I am from the U.S.and I have been playing drums for about 8 years now! I started Streaming for 3 Reasons

  • I have always wanted to find a way to influence people in a positive way. That is why I am so thankful for the success that I have had with this stream! I love the fact that I can share my time with others, meet new people and put a smile on their face, all while getting more proficient with my favorite instrument. (Which leads me to my second point)

  • I LOVE DRUMMING! It is my favorite hobby and I am so happy that I have the chance to play for others! I want to learn new Genres of music and too become much more versatile in the styles that I can play.

  • I wanted to create a positive Environment that people can come together and collaborate their ideas and hang out with each other! I hope that through this stream, I will be able to meet and collab with some of my favorite musicians from across the world. Hopefully, One day, I will be able to play at different conventions and venues under the name "The8BitDrummer"

What I Do

I am a improv drummer that plays drums to a range of soundtracks. BTW if you want you can fill this in :p

The Types of music I love to play to are: Video Game Music, Electronic Music, Dubstep, Rock, Random Internet Songs, ...and Much more!

I am also a Christian and play for different Churches around my home town! If you have any Questions about why, Please, Get in touch with me :D Thanks so much for stopping by and Chilling with me in my little corner of the internet! Have An Awesome Day! :D :D
